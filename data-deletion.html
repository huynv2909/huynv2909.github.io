Data Deletion Policy

This policy outlines my practices for deleting personal data that I collect and process.

Scope

This policy applies to all personal data I collect and process, including but not limited to:

  • name
  • address
  • location
  • pictures

Data Retention

I will retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

  • Contact information will be retained for 1 year after the last interaction

Data Deletion

When personal data is no longer needed, I will delete it securely and permanently.

This includes:

  • Deleting data from my email server.
  • Deleting data from my hard drive.
  • Anonymizing data so it is no longer identifiable.

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
